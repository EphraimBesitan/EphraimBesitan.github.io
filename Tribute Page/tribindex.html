Tribute to Boycot Lais Lapniten

About Boycot Lais Lapniten

Boycot Lais Lapniten was born on October 2, 1930.

Barely touched by education but continues to live as a young farmer back in the day.

In his teens to young adulthood, he traveled around the provinces of Cordillera to seek his fortune other than farming.

There he found himself in a group of cowboys that he ended up joining in.

After a while he realized that he can't leave his roots behind, carrying his cowboy knowledge he went back home to his home town.

In his way home he meet a young beautiful lady named Dalma who he soon marry and had children.

He grew old to be a loving grandfather, father, brother, husband, uncle, etc.

But life was unpredictable sometimes and the end of Boycots life is no excuse.

At the age of 84, Boycot died after suffering a cardiac arrest due to stroke.

Some of his adventures are still a mystery, but all we know is that he lived a fulfilling life and touched the hearts of many.

He will always be remembered for his kindness, wisdom, and the love he shared with his family and friends.
